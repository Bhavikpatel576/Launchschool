Pears and Bacon Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

2 tablespoons unsalted butter

2 tablespoons currant or fig jam

2 slices white sandwitch bread

2 slices Cheddar

2 slices cooked bacon

1/4 small pear, thinkly sliced

Preparations

  1. Melt the butter in a small skillet over medium-low heat. Spread the jam on 1 slice of bread and form a sandwich with the cheese , bacon , and pear .

  2. Cook the sandwich, covered, until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
